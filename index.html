Welcome to Carolina Community CIC Free Shop

a place where the community comes together to help each other by sharing. Whether you are in need of essentials or have items to give, we are here to support one another with care and dignity. A community space where items are shared freely so families can access what they need with dignity.

Carolina Community CIC was created to support families and individuals in Ashford through practical community sharing. Carolinas Ashford Free Shop offers , toys, household items, furniture and food support for those who need it. Everything is provided freely, with the aim of creating a welcoming space where people can both give and receive without judgement. We believe that when communities come together to share resources, everyone benefits.

What We Offer

Clothing for adults and children, toys and children’s items, small household items, furniture (when available), food support At Carolinas Ashford Free Shop we believe in dignity, kindness, community support, reducing waste through sharing. Everyone is welcome.
